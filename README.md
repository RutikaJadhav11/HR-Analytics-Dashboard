#  HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify key factors affecting employee turnover. The dashboard helps HR teams make data-driven decisions.

## Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1470  
- Attrition Count: 237  
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%  
- Average Age: 37  
- Average Years at Company: 7  

## Key Insights
- Highest attrition observed in Research & Development department
- Employees aged 26–35 have the highest attrition
- Laboratory Technician and Sales Executive roles show maximum attrition
- Employees with 5–10 years experience are more likely to leave
- Overtime employees show higher attrition
- Low salary group has highest attrition rate
